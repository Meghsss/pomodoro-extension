# Privacy Policy

Pomodoro Timer Extension does not collect, transmit, or share any personal or sensitive user data.

All settings, timer states, and statistics are stored locally on the user’s device using Chrome’s local storage APIs. No data is sent to external servers, third parties, or analytics services.

The extension does not track browsing history, websites visited, user activity, or personal information.
